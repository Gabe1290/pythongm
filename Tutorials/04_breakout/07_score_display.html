Step 6: Display Score and Lives (Blockly)

Let's add an on-screen display so the player can see their score and remaining lives.

Add the Draw Event

  1. Open obj_game_controller in the Blockly editor (if not already open)
  2. You should see the Create Event from the previous step
  3. Now add a second event block:
Draw Event
Draw score at x: 10 y: 10
Draw lives at x: 200 y: 10

From Events, drag a Draw Event block. From Score/Lives/Health, snap two display blocks inside:

  • Draw score at x: 10 y: 10 — shows the score in the top-left corner
  • Draw lives at x: 200 y: 10 — shows the remaining lives

Apply Your Blocks

  1. You should now have 2 event blocks for obj_game_controller: the Create Event and the Draw Event
  2. Click Apply to Events to save
How it works: The Draw Event runs every frame and paints the score and lives text on screen. As the player breaks bricks, the score goes up. When the ball falls, the lives count goes down.
