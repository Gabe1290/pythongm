Phase 1: Create the Room — Let's Play!

Time to build the room and test your game! We'll place walls around the edges, a death zone at the bottom, a paddle, and the ball.

Create the Room

  1. Right-click on Rooms and select Create New Room
  2. Name it room_breakout
  3. Set size to 640×480 pixels

Room Layout

S T T T T T T T T T T T T T T T T T T S
S                                     S
S                                     S
S                                     S
S                                     S
S                                     S
S                                     S
S                (o)                  S
S                                     S
S            [==PADDLE==]             S
D D D D D D D D D D D D D D D D D D D D

S = Side Wall (obj_wall_side)
T = Top Wall (obj_wall_top)
D = Death Zone (obj_death_zone, invisible)
(o) = Ball

Place the Objects

1. Side Walls

Place obj_wall_side along the left and right edges, from top to bottom. Stack them vertically to form solid columns.

2. Top Wall

Place obj_wall_top along the top edge, between the side walls.

3. Death Zone

Place obj_death_zone along the bottom edge. It will be invisible during gameplay — it catches the ball when it falls.

Important: There is NO wall at the bottom! The open bottom is where the ball can escape. The invisible death zone detects when that happens.

4. Paddle

Place obj_paddle near the bottom of the room, centered horizontally.

5. Ball

Place obj_ball just above the paddle, near the center of the room.

Test Your Game!

Press F5 to play!

  • Use Left and Right arrow keys to move the paddle
  • The ball should bounce off the walls and the paddle
  • When the ball falls past the paddle, it respawns in the center

If something isn't working, go back and check your Blockly blocks. Make sure you clicked Apply to Events for both the paddle and the ball!
