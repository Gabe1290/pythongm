LUNAR LANDER

Control your thrust and land safely on the pad!

What We'll Create

In this tutorial, you'll build a classic lunar lander game step by step. The game is playable at every phase — you test early and add features as you go!

Three Phases

Phase 1: A Flying Lander
Create sprites and objects, add gravity and controls. Fly your lander around the screen!
Phase 2: Landing and Crashing
Add a landing pad and collision detection. Land on the pad to win, crash into the ground to restart!
Phase 3: Game Controller
Add a HUD with title text and instructions. Complete the game!
Time Required: About 20–25 minutes
