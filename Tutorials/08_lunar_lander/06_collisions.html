Step 5: Collision Detection (Blockly)

Handle landing on the pad and crashing into terrain.

Landing on the Pad

Still in the Blockly editor for obj_lander:

When colliding with obj_pad
Stop movement
Show Message "Landing successful! You win!"

Find Collision in Events, type obj_pad. Add Stop Movement (from Movement) to halt the lander, then Show Message (from Game) with your victory text.

Crashing into Terrain

When colliding with obj_ground
Show Message "Crashed into terrain!"
Restart Room

Find Collision in Events, type obj_ground. Add Show Message and Restart Room (from Room). Hitting the rocky ground resets the level!

Apply Your Blocks

  1. You should have 7 event blocks total (Create + 3 keyboard + No key + 2 collision)
  2. Click the Apply to Events button to save
The challenge: Land gently on the pad — don't crash into the ground! Use short bursts of thrust (tap Up) to slow your descent. Touch the pad to win.
