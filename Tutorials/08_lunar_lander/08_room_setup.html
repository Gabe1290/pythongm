Step 7: Room Setup

Design your lunar landscape!

Create the Room

  1. Right-click Rooms > Create Room
  2. Name it room_game
  3. Set size: 640 x 480
  4. Set background color to black (space)
  5. Enable "Snap to Grid" (32x32)

Example Level Layout

        L                          <- Lander starts here





GGG     GGG     PPPP     GGG     GGG
GGGGGGGGGGGGGGGGGGGGGGGGGGGGGGGGGGG

G = Ground (crash)    L = Lander    P = Landing Pad (safe)

Building Your Level

  1. Ground: Place obj_ground along the bottom
  2. Gap: Leave space for the landing pad
  3. Landing Pad: Place obj_pad in the gap
  4. Lander: Place obj_lander at the top
  5. Controller: Place obj_game_controller anywhere

Test Your Game!

Press F5 or click Run!

  • UP Arrow: Thrust (uses fuel!)
  • LEFT/RIGHT: Steer horizontally
  • Watch your speed - stay under 2!
  • Don't run out of fuel!
  • Land on the pad to win!
Congratulations! You've created a lunar lander with gravity, thrust, and fuel management!
Challenge: Add multiple landing pads with different point values, or add wind effects!
