PLATFORMER GAME

Jump across platforms, avoid hazards, collect coins!

What We'll Create

In this tutorial, you'll build a classic platformer game:

  • A player affected by gravity that falls down
  • Jumping mechanics with ground detection
  • Platforms to stand on and jump between
  • Coins to collect for points
  • Spikes that hurt the player
  • A flag to complete the level

What You'll Learn

  • Gravity and falling physics
  • Jump mechanics with ground detection
  • Platform collision (landing on top)
  • Left/right movement controls
  • Collectibles and hazards
