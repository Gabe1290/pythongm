Sokoban: Box-Pushing Puzzle

📦

SOKOBAN

Push all the crates onto the target spots!

What We'll Create

In this tutorial, you'll build a classic Sokoban puzzle game:

  • A player that moves on a grid (one step at a time)
  • Crates that the player can push (but not pull)
  • Target spots where crates need to be placed
  • Win condition when all crates are on targets

What You'll Learn

  • Grid-based movement (moving in fixed steps)
  • Push mechanics - detecting and moving other objects
  • Checking multiple conditions before moving
  • Win condition detection
  • Level design for puzzle games
Time Required: About 30-35 minutes
