Step 6: Set Up the Room

Create a puzzle level with walls, crates, and targets.

Create the Room

  1. Right-click on Rooms and select Create Room
  2. Name it room_sokoban
  3. Set size to 320x320 pixels (10x10 grid)
  4. Enable grid snap: 32x32

Example Level Layout

W W W W W W W W W W
W . . . . . . . . W
W . P . . . C . . W
W . . W W . . . . W
W . . W T . . C . W
W . . . . . W W . W
W . T . . . . . . W
W . . . . . . . . W
W W W W W W W W W W

W = Wall     T = Target
P = Player   C = Crate
. = Empty

Place Objects

1. Walls

Build walls around the edges and create obstacles inside.

2. Targets

Place obj_target where crates should end up.

3. Crates

Place obj_crate - same number as targets!

4. Player

Place obj_player at the starting position.

5. Game Controller

Place obj_game_controller anywhere.

Important: Number of crates MUST equal number of targets!
Congratulations! Your Sokoban game is ready!

Test Your Game

  • Press F5 or click Run
  • Use Arrow Keys to move
  • Push all crates onto the red targets to win!
