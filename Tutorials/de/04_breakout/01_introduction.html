BREAKOUT

Break all the bricks with your ball and paddle!

What We'll Create

In this tutorial, you'll build a classic Breakout game:

  • A paddle that moves left and right with arrow keys
  • A ball that bounces around the screen
  • Rows of colorful bricks to destroy
  • Score tracking as you break bricks
  • Lives system - lose a life when the ball falls

What You'll Learn

  • Keyboard controls for paddle movement
  • Ball bouncing mechanics
  • Destroying objects on collision
  • Score and lives tracking
  • Using parent objects for similar bricks
Time Required: About 25-30 minutes
