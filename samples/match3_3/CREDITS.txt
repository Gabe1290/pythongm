Sprite art and sound effects in this sample — License notice
==============================================================

The four gem images are copied unmodified from samples/match3_2 (same
license, same author). Two of the five sound effects are also copied from
match3_2; snd_special.wav and snd_level_up.wav are new for this sample.
All are dedicated to the public domain by the pygm2 author (Gabriel
Thullen):

  sprites/spr_gem_red.png      (from match3_2)
  sprites/spr_gem_blue.png     (from match3_2)
  sprites/spr_gem_green.png    (from match3_2)
  sprites/spr_gem_yellow.png   (from match3_2)
  sounds/snd_swap.wav          (from match3_2)
  sounds/snd_match.wav         (from match3_2)
  sounds/snd_cascade.wav       (from match3_2)
  sounds/snd_special.wav       (new — plays when a special tile is created
                                 or activated)
  sounds/snd_level_up.wav      (new — plays when a level's target score is
                                 reached and the game advances rooms)

Notes on the new sounds: same synthesis approach as match3_2 — short
(<=0.32s) synthesized tones (sine waves with a linear fade envelope)
written directly via Python's stdlib `wave` module, no recordings or
external sound libraries.
