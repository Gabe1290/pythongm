views_2 — asset credits
=======================

Sprites (spr_player1.png, spr_player2.png, spr_wall.png, spr_coin.png):
  Simple flat-color 32x32 art generated programmatically for this sample.
  Released under CC0 1.0 (public domain dedication) by the pygm2 author.
  spr_wall.png and spr_coin.png are shared with the views_1 sample;
  spr_player1.png (orange) and spr_player2.png (teal) distinguish the two
  split-screen players. No third-party assets are used in this sample.

No sound or music files are bundled with this sample.
