Notes on the art: each gem sprite is a plain 88x88 rounded-square PNG
(generated procedurally with Pillow — no external image assets), sized to
drop into the board exactly where match3_1's colored rectangle used to be,
since the runtime's `draw_sprite` command blits at native pixel size with
no scaling. A center-icon shape (circle/diamond/square/triangle) is layered
on top of each color as a colorblind-safe secondary cue, not just hue.

Notes on the sounds: three short synthesized tones (<=0.3s, sine waves with
a linear fade envelope) written directly via Python's stdlib `wave` module —
no recordings, no external sound libraries.
